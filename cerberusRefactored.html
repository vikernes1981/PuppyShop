About Us

At Cerberus: Adoption Agency for Puppies from Hell, our mission is to match loyal, spirited hellhounds with loving homes on Earth. We aim to provide a safe, nurturing environment where these unique companions can thrive and bring joy to their new families.

Our vision is to create a world where every hellhound finds a devoted human partner, forging bonds that are both fiery and unbreakable. We strive to break stereotypes, showcasing the warmth and loyalty of these extraordinary pups while ensuring their well-being and happiness.
